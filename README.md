### How to Play:

- **Move**: Use **AD** to control your character.
  - **A** to move left.
  - **D** to move right.

- **Jump**: Press **Space** to jump while on the ground.
- **Dash**: Hold **Shift** to dash and increase speed.
- **Restart**: Press **R** to restart the game or level.
